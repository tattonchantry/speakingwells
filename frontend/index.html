What is SpeakingWells?

Many people are non-verbal, they communicate differently, but they notice the world around them just as deeply as anyone else. SpeakingWells gives them a beautifully simple card to hand to anyone they meet.

When someone receives a card, they can scan a QR code to send a message back. That message goes straight to the family's inbox.
